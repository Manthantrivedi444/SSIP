About Us

DMA is a comprehensive web-based application designed to help communities prepare for, respond to, and recover from natural disasters. Our platform provides real-time alerts, resource coordination, and emergency planning tools to ensure safety and resilience in the face of catastrophic events.

Our Mission

At DMA, our mission is to empower communities with the tools and information they need to effectively manage disaster situations. We believe that with proper preparation and coordination, we can significantly reduce the impact of natural disasters on lives and property.

Our application focuses on three key areas:

  • Early warning systems and predictive analytics
  • Resource management and logistics coordination
  • Community engagement and emergency education
